NEXT — HOW YOU STAY IN CONTROL
The contract

You're always in control.

An agent that touches money has to earn its keep. Three rules we never break — you can change everything else.

i.

Transparent

Every action is logged with a plain-English reason. You can see exactly what I did, when, and why — going back as far as you'd like.

ii.

Reversible

Anything I do — every email, every reroute, every flag — can be undone in one click. The undo log lives next to the action.

iii.

Permissioned

I only act inside the rules you set. Sending payments, releasing holds, and high-risk actions always pause for your approval.
